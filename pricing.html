Pricing

Minecraft: Education Edition has a completely different pricing scheme than standard Minecraft. Any organization can purchase licenses for Minecraft: Education Edition, including eligible Academic institutions, other Organizations (like camps, clubs, and homeschools), and even individuals.

Academic licenses are only available to eligible academic institutions. To see if your organization is eligible to purchase academic licenses, check your organizational email at the Office 365 Education page.

Eligible academic institutions can purchase individual licenses for Minecraft: Education Edition directly from Microsoft or can connect with a partner for Volume Licensing.

Any Organization can purchase a commercial license. This is the license option available to any organization (like a camp, club, after-school program, etc.) that is not an eligible academic institution. It is also available to individuals and homeschoolers. See Purchase Commercial Licenses for more information on purchasing commercial licenses.

If you are part of an eligible educational (Acedemic) institution, Minecraft: Education Edition costs $7.16 NZD per user, per year. If you are not part of an eligible educational institution (commercial/Organisation), the cost is $17.17 NZD per user, per year. For more information on how to purchase Minecraft: Education Edition licenses, see Purchasing Options for Minecraft: Education Edition.
